Travel Roulette — Spin the Globe, Discover Your Next Adventure

Travel Roulette is a free 3D interactive globe that randomly picks travel destinations from 1,157 curated places worldwide. Spin the globe and get AI-powered itineraries, booking links for flights and hotels, and destination highlights.

How it works

Pull the arrow handle and release it to spin the 3D Earth globe. It lands on a random destination — from Tokyo to Kotor — with travel highlights, a day-by-day AI itinerary, and one-tap booking for flights, hotels, and experiences.

Features

  • 1,157 curated destinations across every continent
  • AI-powered day-by-day travel itineraries
  • One-tap booking: flights, hotels, experiences, transfers, car rental
  • Group trip rooms with shared voting
  • Themed destination packs: Romantic Europe, Beach Escape, Digital Nomad, Foodie Cities
